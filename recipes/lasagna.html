Lasagna

Description

This recipe for lasagna may take a bit of work, but it is one of the most popular on the internet for a reason! Create a delicious dish to feed your family or serve guests, and watch their jaws drop in awe of the spectacular flavor!

NOTE: This recipe can take around three hours to complete, so be sure to prepare accordingly!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • .75 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • .5 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • .5 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • .25 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • .75 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • .75 cup grated Parmesan cheese
