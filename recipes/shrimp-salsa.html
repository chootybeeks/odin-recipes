Shrimp Salsa

Description

This delicious and easy-to make recipe is great for parties! Made with very simple ingredients, your party guests will love it!

Feel free to spice up the recipe with your own touch by adding things such as jalapenos,black beans, or corn!

Ingredients

  • .5 pound salad shrimp
  • 2 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • .5 red onion, diced
  • .25 cup minced cilantro
  • .25 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic minced

Steps

  1. Stir the salad shrimp, tomatioes, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a large glass bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until the flavors combine, at least one hour. Serve cold.
